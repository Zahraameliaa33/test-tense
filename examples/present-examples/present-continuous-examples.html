Present Continuous Examples

  • (+) I am studying English right now.
  • (-) I am not studying English right now.
  • (?) Am I studying English right now?
  • (+) You are working hard at school now.
  • (-) You are not working hard at school now.
  • (?) Are you working hard at school now?
  • (+) They are playing football in the yard.
  • (-) They are not playing football in the yard.
  • (?) Are they playing football in the yard?
  • (+) We are going to the park at the moment.
  • (-) We are not going to the park at the moment.
  • (?) Are we going to the park at the moment?
  • (+) She is watching a movie now.
  • (-) She is not watching a movie now.
  • (?) Is she watching a movie now?
  • (+) He is reading a book at the moment.
  • (-) He is not reading a book at the moment.
  • (?) Is he reading a book at the moment?
  • (+) It is raining outside now.
  • (-) It is not raining outside now.
  • (?) Is it raining outside now?
