Present Simple Examples

  • (+) I study English every day.
  • (-) I do not study English every day.
  • (?) Do I study English every day?
  • (+) You work hard at school.
  • (-) You do not work hard at school.
  • (?) Do you work hard at school?

  • (-) They do not play football on weekends.
  • (?) Do they play football on weekends?
  • (+) We go to the park every Sunday.
  • (-) We do not go to the park every Sunday.
  • (?) Do we go to the park every Sunday?
  • (+) She watches a movie every Friday.
  • (-) She does not watch a movie every Friday.
  • (?) Does she watch a movie every Friday?
  • (+) He reads a book in the evening.
  • (-) He does not read a book in the evening.
  • (?) Does he read a book in the evening?
  • (+) It rains a lot in spring.
  • (-) It does not rain a lot in spring.
  • (?) Does it rain a lot in spring?
