Present Perfect Examples

  • (+) I have finished my homework.
  • (-) I have not finished my homework.
  • (?) Have I finished my homework?
  • (+) You have visited London before.
  • (-) You have not visited London before.
  • (?) Have you visited London before?
  • (+) They have eaten lunch already.
  • (-) They have not eaten lunch yet.
  • (?) Have they eaten lunch already?
  • (+) We have seen that movie.
  • (-) We have not seen that movie yet.
  • (?) Have we seen that movie?
  • (+) She has finished her assignment.
  • (-) She has not finished her assignment yet.
  • (?) Has she finished her assignment?
  • (+) He has gone to the store.
  • (-) He has not gone to the store yet.
  • (?) Has he gone to the store?
  • (+) It has rained a lot this week.
  • (-) It has not rained much this week.
  • (?) Has it rained a lot this week?
