Future Perfect Examples

  • (+) I will have visited my grandparents by next weekend.
  • (-) I will not have visited my grandparents by next weekend.
  • (?) Will I have visited my grandparents by next weekend?
  • (+) You will have watched the movie by tomorrow evening.
  • (-) You will not have watched the movie by tomorrow evening.
  • (?) Will you have watched the movie by tomorrow evening?
  • (+) They will have finished their homework by 6 PM.
  • (-) They will not have finished their homework by 6 PM.
  • (?) Will they have finished their homework by 6 PM?
  • (+) We will have gone to the park by next Sunday.
  • (-) We will not have gone to the park by next Sunday.
  • (?) Will we have gone to the park by next Sunday?
  • (+) She will have watched a movie by tomorrow evening.
  • (-) She will not have watched a movie by tomorrow evening.
  • (?) Will she have watched a movie by tomorrow evening?
  • (+) He will have played football with his friends by Saturday.
  • (-) He will not have played football with his friends by Saturday.
  • (?) Will he have played football with his friends by Saturday?
  • (+) It will have rained by tomorrow morning.
  • (-) It will not have rained by tomorrow morning.
  • (?) Will it have rained by tomorrow morning?
