Future Continuous Examples

  • (-) I will not be studying at 6 PM tomorrow.
  • (?) Will I be studying at 6 PM tomorrow?

  • (?) Will you be working on the project at 5 PM?

  • (?) Will they be playing football at noon?
  • (+) We will be traveling at midnight.
  • (-) We will not be traveling at midnight.
  • (?) Will we be traveling at midnight?

  • (?) Will she be reading at 8 PM tomorrow?
