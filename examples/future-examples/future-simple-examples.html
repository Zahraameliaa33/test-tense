Future Simple Examples

  • (+) I will visit my grandparents next weekend.
  • (-) I will not visit my grandparents next weekend.
  • (?) Will I visit my grandparents next weekend?
  • (+) You will watch the movie tomorrow.
  • (-) You will not watch the movie tomorrow.
  • (?) Will you watch the movie tomorrow?
  • (+) They will finish their homework on time.
  • (-) They will not finish their homework on time.
  • (?) Will they finish their homework on time?
  • (+) We will go to the park next Sunday.
  • (-) We will not go to the park next Sunday.
  • (?) Will we go to the park next Sunday?
  • (+) She will watch a movie tomorrow evening.
  • (-) She will not watch a movie tomorrow evening.
  • (?) Will she watch a movie tomorrow evening?
  • (+) He will play football with his friends on Saturday.
  • (-) He will not play football with his friends on Saturday.
  • (?) Will he play football with his friends on Saturday?
  • (+) It will rain tomorrow.
  • (-) It will not rain tomorrow.
  • (?) Will it rain tomorrow?
