Past Simple Examples

  • (+) I visited my grandparents last weekend.
  • (-) I did not visit my grandparents last weekend.
  • (?) Did I visit my grandparents last weekend?
  • (+) You watched the movie yesterday.
  • (-) You did not watch the movie yesterday.
  • (?) Did you watch the movie yesterday?
  • (+) They finished their homework on time.
  • (-) They did not finish their homework on time.
  • (?) Did they finish their homework on time?
  • (+) We went to the park last Sunday.
  • (-) We did not go to the park last Sunday.
  • (?) Did we go to the park last Sunday?
  • (+) She watched a movie yesterday evening.
  • (-) She did not watch a movie yesterday evening.
  • (?) Did she watch a movie yesterday evening?
  • (+) He played football with his friends on Saturday.
  • (-) He did not play football with his friends on Saturday.
  • (?) Did he play football with his friends on Saturday?
  • (+) It rained yesterday.
  • (-) It did not rain yesterday.
  • (?) Did it rain yesterday?
