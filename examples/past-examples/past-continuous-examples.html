Past Continuous Examples

  • (+) I was reading a book at 8 PM.
  • (-) I was not reading a book at 8 PM.
  • (?) Was I reading a book at 8 PM?
  • (+) You were playing football yesterday.
  • (-) You were not playing football yesterday.
  • (?) Were you playing football yesterday?
  • (+) They were studying for exams yesterday.
  • (-) They were not studying for exams yesterday.
  • (?) Were they studying for exams yesterday?
  • (+) She was listening to music at that time.
  • (-) She was not listening to music at that time.
  • (?) Was she listening to music at that time?
  • (+) He was cooking dinner when I arrived.
  • (-) He was not cooking dinner when I arrived.
  • (?) Was he cooking dinner when I arrived?
  • (+) It was raining all afternoon.
  • (-) It was not raining all afternoon.
  • (?) Was it raining all afternoon?
